Who We Are

A Community-Focused Nonprofit Rooted in Compassion

Govind Foundation is dedicated to promoting well-being, compassion, and environmental responsibility through programs that serve both people and animals.

We host retreats that give members of the public the opportunity to step away from urban life and experience the calm and restorative benefits of rural environments — supporting mental well-being, community connection, and inclusive shared experiences.

From our animal shelter to our environmental stewardship programs, everything we do is guided by a single belief: that a more compassionate and sustainable future is possible when we work together.

Our Three Missions

What We Stand For

Our Three Core Missions

Every program and initiative we run is guided by these three pillars.

☮

Peace & Humanity

We support community well-being by hosting inclusive events and retreats that bring people together, promote peaceful coexistence, and foster a sense of belonging for all.

🐄

Animal Protection

We provide care, safety, and shelter for horses, cows, and other rescued or at-risk animals — with ongoing plans to expand our rescue and rehabilitation efforts over time.

🌿

Environmental Stewardship

Through local education on sustainable farming and community tree-planting initiatives, we work to enhance ecological health and build long-term environmental sustainability.

What We Do

Our Programs

Rural Retreats

Our retreats invite the public to unplug from city life and immerse themselves in the quiet rhythm of farm living. Designed to restore mental well-being and build genuine human connection.

Animal Shelter & Rescue

Our shelter is a safe haven for horses, cows, and other animals in need. We provide ongoing care and a nurturing environment while expanding our rescue and rehabilitation capacity.

Environmental Education

We run community-led tree planting of flowering and fruit-bearing trees, paired with local education on sustainable farming practices to build lasting ecological awareness.
